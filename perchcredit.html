Perch Credit
Client: Perch (Formally Hatch Credit)
Year: 2019-2020
Role: UX Designer
Earlier this year, I worked with the Hatch Credit team, now Perch, to design an iOS app that helps build credit through cost tracking.

What I Did

I helped to design the monthly expense tracking feature for Hatch Credit. I gained experience working with Figma and Zeplin in a collaborative design setting to create high-fidelity prototypes.

TAKEAWAY

This was one of my first experiences with UX design and it was amazing to see the app development process. I look forward to designing and building more apps for startups.
