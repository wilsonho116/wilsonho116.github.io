CariClub

Overview

CariClub is an online platform that matches corporate employees with non-profit boards. I was the sole UX and graphic design intern at CariClub. As a UX designer, I worked on redesigning the employee profile page to be more accessible and efficient to use. As a graphic designer, I worked directly with the CEO to create slide decks for potential investors, promotional graphics, and system graphics describing the CariClub ecosystem. Currently, any designs that have not been implemented or contain sensitive information are confidential. Please reach out to me via email if you would like to see more!

System Graphic

Non-profit board matching can be a complicated system at first glance. My design goal for this graphic was to make non-profit and associate board matching understandable for everyone.

Promotional Graphics

These graphics were posted on CariClub's social media to promote the company and encourage corporate employees to take part in social action.

Informational Graphics

These graphics are meant to be more detailed and were used to convey more information to support the efficacy and credibility of CariClub.
