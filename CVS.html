About Project

This semester, I am working with DFA national and CVS to strategize and design a solution for sustainable health and wellness. CVS is the largest healthcare company and is focused on becoming the most human-centric. With this goal in mind, my team and I are looking to challenge the product life cycle by rethinking how products are devliered to customers.
