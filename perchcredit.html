ABOUT PERCH CREDIT

Perch Credit is a mobile app that allows users to report rent, phone bills, online subscriptions, and more to start building their credit score without any prior experience.

MY GOAL

I joined the Perch team in the middle of the their initial development, however my goal was to take a new look on monthly cost tracking.

The Challenge

Because this company was quite new in its development, there was an emphasis on function over look. I had 3 design goals:

1. Intuitively visualize cost and credit tracking.

2. Use easy-to-understand features to understand spending within common categories.

3. Create a sense of familiarity with the way the app works, basing a lot of the features on other cost-tracking apps.

THE DESIGN

The Main Features:

1. Credit + Cost Tracking: Show users their monthly spending based on certain categories. Help users to save on monthly costs and build credit.
