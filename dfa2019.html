ABOUT PROJECT

Last year, my team at USC Design for America reserached minority rights within the space of local small businesses in Los Angeles.

Our mission is to provide an interactive platform for small food business to market their products, share their stories, and provide live updates. So far, we have done some extensive research around the community.

Our Research Method

We interviewed and got to know many local restaurants around south-LA, an area that has struggled with poverty and violence. However, some of the best food is found here and USC students know that. We wanted to know what small-food-businesses in the area were struggling with and how we could help.

Research Takeaway

We found that customers and customer experience are centered around a shared culture. However, one of the biggest issues small businesses face is lack of marketing. Many small businesse rely on word-of-mouth to maintain their customer base.

Our Idea

Because social media outlets are often integral but inaccessible forms of marketing for local small businesses, we are looking to create an easy-to-use platform for small businesses to interact with their customers.
