ABOUT MAJOR LEAGUE MARKETS

Major League Markets is an app that allows users to trade and bet sports-stock, all while keeping up-to-date on the latest news.

MY GOAL

I joined Major League Markets at the beginning of its development. My goal was to build the layout for the features we were trying to achieve.

The Challenge

Major League Markets was just being created when I joined the team, so I wanted to design prototypes that could be used for changing functionalities in the future.

1. Provide an intuitive platform for users to trade, buy, and sell sport stock.

2. Inform users on the latest news in sports as well as personalized information based on interests.

THE DESIGN

The Main Features:

1. Invest + Follow: Allow users to easily invest and track their favorite teams' standings.

2. Inform: Provide users with team-specific information.
