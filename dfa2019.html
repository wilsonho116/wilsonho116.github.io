ABOUT DESIGN FOR AMERICA

Design for America is an idea incubator, a way of approaching complex challenges, and a life-long community of learners.

MY GOAL

In 2019, we noticed an increasingly gentrified area around the USC campus. After we researched further, we realized that small minority businesses were struggling to resist being pushed out of their own communities.

The Challenge

Our mission was to provide an interactive platform for small food businesses to market their products, share their stories, and provide live updates.

1. Better understand the community's struggles and analyze pain points.

2. Design an interface that intuitively allows local business owners to market their products.

THE DESIGN

From extensive secondary research we noticed a couple key points.

1. Local food businesses use, almost solely, word-of-mouth communication to reach out to their customer base.
